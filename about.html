About Us

RedZone first opened its doors in 1972 by a man named Howard Jenkins. It started in a small hangar at the Charlotte Douglas International Airport. RedZone has since then grown and now has 3 locations across the US (Los Angeles, Charlotte, and Wichita). We love skydiving with a passion and we have jumped with over 10,000 customers!
