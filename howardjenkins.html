Howard Jenkins

My Name is Howard Jenkins. I was born in 1963 Febuary 12th. I am the owner and the creater of Redzone skydiving. My job here is to make sure that you have a good time and that our equipment is up to date and that everything is safe to use. I have a wife and two kids. I grew up in the east where all I did was study hard and to be able to get where I am today. My dad was a pilot and he took me to air shows for my birthday every year and I loved it. So I wouldn't be here today if my dad did not introduce to me to such an amazing thing to do.
